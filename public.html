Biography

Playboi Carti, born Jordan Terrell Carter, is an American rapper, singer, and record producer known for his distinct sound and innovative approach to hip-hop. Hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, Carti has rapidly gained recognition for his minimalist beats, catchy hooks, and a style that pushes the boundaries of conventional rap.

Music

Carti burst onto the music scene with hit singles like "Magnolia" and the debut album "Die Lit." His experimental style and unique vocal delivery have captivated a global audience. With his follow-up album "Whole Lotta Red," he further solidified his status as a pioneering artist in the trap and mumble rap genres.
