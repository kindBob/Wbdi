Music

Carti burst onto the music scene with hit singles like "Magnolia" and the debut album "Die Lit." His experimental style and unique vocal delivery have captivated a global audience. With his follow-up album "Whole Lotta Red," he further solidified his status as a pioneering artist in the trap and mumble rap genres.
