Biography

Playboi Carti, born Jordan Terrell Carter, is an American rapper, singer, and record producer known for his distinct sound and innovative approach to hip-hop. Hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, Carti has rapidly gained recognition for his minimalist beats, catchy hooks, and a style that pushes the boundaries of conventional rap.

Growing up in Atlanta, Carti was influenced by the city's rich hip-hop culture. His early exposure to trap music and the city's vibrant music scene helped shape his unique artistic direction.

Carti's rise to fame began with his self-titled debut mixtape in 2017, which featured the breakout hit "Magnolia." His innovative approach to music production and vocal delivery quickly set him apart from his contemporaries.
